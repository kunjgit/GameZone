HOW TO PLAY

FALLING WORDS

is a game where you must type the word as fast as you can !! falling from the top of the blurred square.

If it reaches the bottom cyan line, you lose.

  • GAME LEVELS:
  • EASY - 1 word per 3 sec
  • MEDIUM - 1 word per 2 sec
  • HARD - 1 word per 1 sec
